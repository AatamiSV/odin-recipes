Pasta with pesto

Description

A simple and delicious staple in the italian cousine

Ingredients

  • Any type of pasta
  • Tomato or basil pesto
  • Salt and italian herb mix
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta as instructed on the packaging in salted water
  2. Drain the pasta after its been boiled and place in a dish
  3. Add in pesto, italian seasoning to taste and a spoon of olive oil
  4. Enjoy!
