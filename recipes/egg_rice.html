Egg fried rice

Description

Classic japanese meal

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • Canned peas and carrots
  • Green onions
  • 1 packet of rice
  • Soy sauce

Steps

  1. Cook rice as instructed on the packaging in salted water
  2. Pour 2 spoons of oil in a wok and fry eggs
  3. After eggs have been fried add peas and carrots and mix them together
  4. Add rice to the wok, mix everything together and add soy sauce
  5. Put in a dish and sprinkle with green onions
  6. Enjoy!
