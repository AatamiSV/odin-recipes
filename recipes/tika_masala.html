Tikka Masala

Description

Classic indian meal

Ingredients

  • Chicken or vegan alternative
  • Tikka Masala sauce
  • 1 packet of rice

Steps

  1. Cook rice as instructed on the packaging in salted water
  2. Fry chicken or vegan alternative in a pan
  3. After the protein has been fried add in rice and stir together
  4. Put rice in a dish and pour the sauce with protein over the rice
  5. Enjoy!
